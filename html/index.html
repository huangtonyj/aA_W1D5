  • 2 cans of Chickpeas (garbanzo beans)
  • 1/2 cup of Tahini
  • 2 Garlic Cloves
  • 1/4 cup of Olive Oil
  • 1/4 cup of Lemon Juice
  • 1/2 teaspoon of Salt

Hummus

Popular Mediterranean dip that is a favorite among hip white vegans. Goes well with pita.

  1. Put chickpeas, tahini, garlic, olive oil, lemon juice, and salt into a food processor or blender.
  2. Blend until creamy
  3. Taste and add salt and olive oil as desired
